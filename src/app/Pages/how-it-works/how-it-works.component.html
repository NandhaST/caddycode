HOW IT WORKS

BOOK A STOCK

Are you moving, renovating or needing space? Renting supplies at
Safe Storage is easy.

  • Choose a place that suits you – perhaps near home or near work.
  • Choose a size that fits your needs. Use our size calculator if you
    need guidance. You can change the size at a later stage.
  • Go to the checkout, choose when you want to move into your store
    and how you want to pay. With us you can pay by card, invoice or
    Swish – the choice is yours.

At Safe Storage there is no complicated paperwork, no binding times and
lots of space. Welcome and good luck!

WHEN YOU BOOK A STOCK

As soon as you book a store, you will immediately receive a personal code via e-mail
and you can easily move in from the day you have selected as move-in day. An account
is also created where you can easily pay your stock every month, cancel your contract
or switch to another store. Most of our facilities are open around the clock so you
can come and go as you please

SAFE STORAGE

Each storage room has a unique lock, which only you have the key to. At all of our
facilities, we have real-time video surveillance – for your safety. We have also
provided all our new facilities with the latest technology and can thus monitor
temperature, lighting, gates and doors. We also see it as a matter of course that
we have clean and tidy stores.
